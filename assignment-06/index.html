Sata Andagi

Original recipe from Just One Cookbook

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1/3 cup black sugar
  • 1 tsp. vegetable oil
  • 1 cup cake flour
  • 1/8 tsp. sea salt
  • 1 tsp. baking powder
  • 3 cups vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. In a bowl combine your egg, 1/3 cup black sugar and whisk together until it's dissolved.
  3. Add your 1 tsp. of oil and whisk together.
  4. Sift your 1 cup cake flour, 1 tsp. baking powder, and 1/8 kosher salt.
  5. Use a spatula to mix the dry and wet ingredients and let the dough rest for an hour.
  6. Bring 3 cups oil to 300-320 degrees.
  7. Grab some oil to rub in your palms to prevent the dough from sticking.
  8. When the oil has reached the proper temperature, scoop the dough and roll into a ball (ping pong size).
  9. Once done rolling, put dough balls in oil (but not too many at once).
  10. When the dough balls start to float and are golden brown, pick them up and drain the rest of the oil using a wire rack or paper towel.
  11. Let cool for 5 minutes and ENJOY!
